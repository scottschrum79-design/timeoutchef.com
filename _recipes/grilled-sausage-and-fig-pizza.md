<p class="pill">Serves 2</p>

## Ingredients

- 1 lb fresh pizza dough,
- homemade or bought
- Parmesan Cheese, grated
- Arugula or other bitter greens
- 2 sausages, fully cooked, sliced
- 6 dried figs or dates, sliced
- 1/2 red onion , sliced , rings
- separated
- 1 cup goat cheese, crumbled

## Directions

1. Prepare barbecue (medium -high heat).
2. Halve dough ; roll two 10-inch rounds on floured work surface .
3. Irregular shapes are fine.
4. In a small bowl mix a Â¼ cup olive oil with a punch of salt and pepper .
5. Brush one side of each pizza dough with oil mixture.
6. Grill pizzas, oiled side down, until golden on bottom, about 4 minutes. Turn pizzas over. Brush again with oil mixture and then sprinkle with parmesan , arugula , sausages , figs, onions , and goat cheese . Grill until pizza is crispy and cheese starts to melt , about 4 minutes.
