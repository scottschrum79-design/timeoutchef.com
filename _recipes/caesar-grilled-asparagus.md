## Ingredients

### Asparagus
- 1 lb fresh asparagus, trimmed  
- 1 tbsp olive oil  
- Salt, to taste  
- Black pepper, to taste  

### Caesar Dressing
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice  
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard  
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce  
- 1 garlic clove, minced  
- 2 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese  
- Salt and pepper, to taste  

### For Serving
- Additional shaved or grated Parmesan  
- Lemon wedges  

---

## Instructions

### 1. Prep the Asparagus
- Preheat grill to medium-high heat.  
- Toss asparagus with olive oil, salt, and pepper until evenly coated.  

### 2. Grill
- Place asparagus directly on the grill grates.  
- Grill for 6–10 minutes, turning occasionally, until tender and lightly charred.  

### 3. Make the Caesar Dressing
- In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, and Parmesan.  
- Season with salt and pepper to taste.  

### 4. Toss and Serve
- Transfer grilled asparagus to a serving platter.  
- Drizzle with Caesar dressing and toss lightly.  
- Top with extra Parmesan and serve with lemon wedges.  

---

## Notes

- For extra flavor, add anchovy paste (½ tsp) to the dressing.  
- Use a grill basket if your asparagus spears are thin.  
- Best served immediately while warm and slightly crisp.
