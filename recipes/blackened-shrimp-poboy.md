<p class="pill">Serves 4</p>

## Ingredients

- 1 lb shrimp, raw, medium,
- thawed, peeled and deveined
- 2 tsp old bay
- 3 TBSP mayonnaise
- 1 TBSP Dijon mustard
- 1 loaf French bread, cut into 4
- sections, each half split and
- excess bread dug out
- 1/2 head romaine lettuce,
- shredded
- 1 yellow or red bell pepper,

## Directions

1. sliced 2 small tomatoes, sliced 2 scallions sliced Preheat a grill or grill pan to medium -high.
2. In a small bowl, combine the shrimp , old bay , and a swirl of olive oil . Toss to coat the shrimp.
3. In another small bowl, combine the mayonnaise , mustard and 1 Â½ teaspoons water , stirring until smooth.
4. Cook the shrimp on the grill or grill pan, until cooked through, about 2 minutes per side .
5. Lay the bread open on plates. Layer with the lettuce , bell pepper , tomatoes and scallions .
6. Arrange the shrimp on top of the vegetables and drizzle with the mayo -mustard sauce.

## Notes

- Old bay seasoning in this recipe can be replaced with a combination of chili powder, garlic powder, salt and pepper. If you would like more of a grilled flavor use smoked paprika instead of old bay and add a pinch of salt. A lot of store bought shrimp is already deveined, check with your store or the package to find out.
